About Us

Since 1974, Interwood has provided a variety of high-quality furniture products, and is today recognized as Pakistan’s leading and most trusted furniture brand.

Interwood's Journey

Interwood Mobel (Pvt.) Ltd. was established in 1974 and has since become Pakistan’s most trusted name in high-quality furniture. Today, it is widely recognized as an industry trendsetter and leader offering a complete range of home and office furniture, kitchens, doors,wardrobes, flooring, accessories and bespoke furniture Starting out with humble roots as a small cabinet and wood workshop in Karachi, Interwood’s quality products quickly gained popularity with a mass audience, exponentially fueling the company’s growth. In less than half a century, Interwood expanded into a major market player with several offices across the country and a retail footprint of 9 showrooms in major metropolitan cities. These showrooms are today considered landmarks in Pakistan’s busiest cities and offer customers a complete variety of furniture products under one roof. The latest showroom, which recently opened in Islamabad in 2019, spans over 55,000 square feet, offering customers an unparalleled retail experience with an extensive range of high-quality furniture solutions. Additionally, Interwood also actively continues to expand its product offerings to customers by introducing new trends and collaborating with international experts (such as Italian architect Alfredo Zengario) to constantly introduce a wider range of products inspired by diverse influences and built to suit individual customers’ needs. To meet the growing market demand, Interwood set up its own dedicated 600,000 square foot factory in Lahore, equipped with a staff of over 1,100 workers and over 300 machines - the most advanced in the country. From wood seasoning and processing, to automated painting, polishing, crafting and finishing, Interwood’s factory is a modern marvel of innovation and technology in the furniture industry and has enabled the company to achieve mass production capability without compromising on its high standard of quality, reducing costs and passing the savings on to its valuable customers. In addition to individual customers, Interwood also caters largely to mega-scale projects providing its trademark furniture and interior-designing solutions to a variety of sectors in Pakistan and abroad. From local luxury residential projects, malls, hospitals, schools and airports, to multi-national corporations, international hotels, and institutions abroad, Interwood continues to make its mark on a variety of businesses and corporations in USA, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East. At the heart of Interwood’s focus remains its expansive base of loyal customers, which can be seen through its dedicated after sales support to all its retail and corporate clients, ensuring customer satisfaction at every level even years after purchase.
